---
title: Create Your First Documentation Page
description: Step-by-step to add a new page in test.
---

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Login to `https://app.test.com` and select your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Docs">
    Access the "Docs" tab from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new page">
    Click "New Page", add a title, and start writing!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your page">
    Click "Save" to publish. You can edit anytime.
  </Step>
</Steps>

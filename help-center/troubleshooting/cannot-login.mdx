---
title: Cannot Login to test
description: What to do if you cannot sign in.
---

## Symptoms
- Login fails
- "Invalid email or password" error

## Solution
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email address">
    Ensure your email is correct. No typos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click "Forgot password?" and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    Still stuck? Email support@test.com for help.
  </Step>
</Steps>
